ABOUT BCRYPT

BCrypt is India’s premier that focuses on Web 3.0, AI, Blockchain, and Emerging technologies. Founders and executives of leading Web3, AI and Blockchain companies and projects share their insights and vision for the future of the space, while attendees learn, network, and collaborate.

Shaping the Future of Web3 and Blockchain

The BCrypt Conference 2024, hosted by C# Corner, successfully concluded on October 18, 2024, at the Radisson Blu Kaushambi in Delhi. This groundbreaking event brought together over 50,000 participants from around the globe, both virtually and in person, to explore the cutting-edge developments in blockchain, Web3, and AI technologies.

A Convergence of Visionaries

The conference featured an impressive lineup of industry leaders and innovators, including Nikil Viswanathan, Co-Founder of Alchemy; Miko Matsumura, Managing Partner at gumi Cryptos Capital; and Phillip Shoemaker, CEO of Identity.com. These thought leaders shared their insights on the future of decentralized technologies, emerging trends in cryptocurrency, and the critical role of digital identity solutions in empowering secure online interactions.

Key Highlights:

  • Engaging Sessions: Attendees participated in keynotes, panels, and discussions covering a wide range of topics such as tokenomics, Web3 security, AI integration in decentralized systems, and the evolving landscape of crypto regulations.

  • Networking Opportunities: The conference provided a platform for participants to connect with peers, industry experts, and potential collaborators, fostering relationships that will drive innovation in the Web3 space.

  • Showcasing Innovation: Leading companies such as Ava Labs, SolanaU, and Capgemini contributed to discussions on how to leverage AI within decentralized frameworks, further emphasizing the intersection of these transformative technologies.


The Sharp Economy’s Role

The Sharp Economy played a crucial role at BCrypt by promoting its innovative ecosystem powered by Sharp Tokens (SHARP). This initiative empowers developers to learn, earn, and grow within a vibrant community that rewards contributions with real tokens. The integration of this model into the conference highlighted the importance of nurturing talent in the rapidly evolving Web3 landscape.

Looking Forward

As we reflect on the success of BCrypt Conference 2024, we are excited about the future of blockchain and Web3 technologies in India. The event not only showcased India’s potential as a leader in these fields but also set the stage for ongoing collaboration among industry stakeholders.

We look forward to continuing this momentum as we prepare for future events that will further advance our mission to build a robust ecosystem for developers and innovators.
