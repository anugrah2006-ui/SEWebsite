A New Collaboration 

We’re excited to share that Sharp Economy is partnering with GDG Hacks to support Web3 and AI education for college students and future developers, entrepreneurs, and tech enthusiasts. This collaboration aims to create opportunities for learning, building, and earning rewards through practical challenges and projects. 

What is GDG Hacks? 

GDG Hacks: Where Innovation Meets Collaboration!

Embark on an extraordinary journey with GDG Hacks, North India’s largest hackathon, powered by the collective brilliance of 8 Google Developer Groups. This isn’t just another hackathon—it’s a movement for developers to unleash their potential, connect with like-minded innovators, and create solutions that matter.

GDG Hacks is more than a hackathon; it’s a platform for growth, connection, and impact. Whether you’re a coding veteran or a first-time participant, this is your chance to innovate, collaborate, and make a difference.

What is Sharp Economy? 

The Sharp Economy represents the first AI-driven growth economy tailored for purpose-oriented communities, brands, and educational institutions. Its primary objective is to foster member growth through innovative models such as Learn2Earn and Spend2Grow. Gamifying continuous learning and member growth is goal of the sharp economy.

The framework of the Sharp Economy is built upon four essential pillars: the Sharp Platform, which serves as a scalable Web 3.0 platform as a service; the Sharp Token, acting as the utility token for community engagement; Sharp Rewards, which incentivize participation; and the Sharp Foundation, dedicated to promoting free education as a fundamental human right.

Why GDG Hacks x Sharp Economy? 

GDG (Google Developer Groups) Hacks focuses on supporting students advancing their career via hackathons and events. By working with Sharp Economy, this partnership will explore the connections between Web3, blockchain, AI, and open-source development, providing participants with new learning and earning opportunities. 

Goals of the Partnership: 

  • Encourage Innovation – Support participants in building projects that use blockchain and AI. 
  • Reward Contributors – Offer Sharp Tokens and other incentives to recognize innovative work. 
  • Expand Community Reach – Strengthen the network of developers and foster knowledge-sharing. 
  • Enhance Skill Development – Provide hands-on experience in blockchain, decentralized applications, and AI-driven solutions. 

What’s Coming? 

  1. Hackathon Challenges 
    Participants at GDG Hacks will be able to take part in Sharp Economy-sponsored challenges, applying blockchain, smart contracts, and AI to build solutions. Selected projects will receive Sharp Tokens, mentorship, and potential funding opportunities. 
  1. Web3 & AI Learning Sessions 
    SharpEconomy will host workshops, webinars, and mentorship sessions to help developers learn and apply new technologies. 
  1. Rewards & Opportunities 
    Through this partnership, GDG Hacks participants and Sharp Economy members will have access to: 
  1. Sharp Merch and other ecosystem benefits. 
  1. Invitations to Sharp Economy and GDG events. 
  1. Networking opportunities with professionals in blockchain, AI, and software development. 

Join Us 

This partnership aims to create opportunities for students and developers to explore new ideas, gain experience, and connect with like-minded individuals. Whether you’re a future developer, designer, or blockchain enthusiast, this is a chance to be part of a growing tech community. 

Stay connected for updates and upcoming challenges!
