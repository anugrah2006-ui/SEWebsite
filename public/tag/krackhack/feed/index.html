What is KrackHack ?

KrackHack is an event where developers, students, and technology enthusiasts collaborate to solve real-world problems. The hackathon provides an opportunity for hands-on learning, networking, and teamwork. Participants will work in teams to create solutions and present their projects to industry professionals.

The hackathon focuses on areas such as artificial intelligence, blockchain, cybersecurity, and software development, allowing participants to gain experience with cutting-edge technologies.
